Important Questions about India

  1. What is the capital of India?
    • Ans ➔ New Delhi
  2. Who was the first Prime Minister of India?
    • Ans ➔ Jawaharlal Nehru
  3. What is the national currency of India?
    • Ans ➔ Indian Rupee (₹)
  4. When did India gain independence?
    • Ans ➔ August 15, 1947
  5. Which river is considered the holiest in India?
    • Ans ➔ The Ganges (Ganga)
  6. What is the national animal of India?
    • Ans ➔ Bengal Tiger
  7. Which is the largest state in India by area?
    • Ans ➔ Rajasthan
  8. What is the national sport of India?
    • Ans ➔ Field Hockey (official) and Cricket (popular)
  9. Which is the most widely spoken language in India?
    • Ans ➔ Hindi
  10. Who is known as the "Father of the Nation"?
    • Ans ➔ Mahatma Gandhi
